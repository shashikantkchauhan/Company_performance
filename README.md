# Company Performance Analysis: Investigating the $1.2M Loss

## 📌 Project Overview
The primary goal of this project is to analyze over 7,000 sales transactions to understand the core drivers of revenue and profit. Despite generating a high total sales volume of **₹ 11,12,975**, the company is operating at a severe net loss of **-₹ 2,06,737**. 

This project investigates the operational inefficiencies and "loss-leader" categories driving this deficit, providing actionable insights for business restructuring.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
1. **Systemic Profitability Issue:** The business is not just struggling in one area; the waterfall chart reveals that *all five* major product categories (Books, Electronics, Fashion, Home Appliances, and Toys) are operating at a loss.
2. **The "Toys" Deficit:** While all categories are bleeding margins, the 'Toys' category is the largest single contributor to the net loss, draining over ₹ 44,475 from the bottom line. 
3. **Volume vs. Value:** Despite having a solid customer base (160 total customers across 130 countries), the pricing strategy or operational costs are entirely misaligned with the sales volume. The company is effectively losing money on every order processed.
